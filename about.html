About Chess Yodha

Welcome to Chess Yodha, your ultimate destination for a challenging and engaging chess experience!

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly and intelligent chess application that caters to players of all skill levels. We believe that chess is more than just a game; it's a powerful tool for developing critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and strategic foresight.

Chess Yodha is designed to offer a clean interface, intuitive controls, and a robust AI opponent that can adapt to your play style (with future difficulty levels coming soon!).

Features

  • Interactive Chessboard with intuitive drag-and-drop moves.
  • Intelligent AI opponent (currently set to Hard difficulty).
  • Move history tracking for reviewing past moves.
  • Undo functionality to correct mistakes or explore variations.
  • Visual indicators for selected pieces, possible moves, and king in check.
  • Responsive design for seamless play on various devices.

Future Enhancements

We are continuously working to improve Chess Yodha. Future updates will include:

  • Multiple AI difficulty levels (Easy, Medium).
  • Customizable board themes and piece styles.
  • Online multiplayer functionality.
  • Tutorials and advanced chess puzzles.
  • User profiles and progress tracking.
